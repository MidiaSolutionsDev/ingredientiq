Methodology

We classify on a 4-level scale: Safe, Caution, Avoid, Unknown, using INCI naming, dermatology literature, and expert consensus.

  • Safe: Strong consensus of tolerability in typical use.
  • Caution: Depends on context (acne-prone, pregnancy, sensitivity).
  • Avoid: Higher irritation risk or insufficient disclosure (“fragrance”).
  • Unknown: Not enough evidence or not in our dictionary.
